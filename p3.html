Big Data helps businesses develop new growth opportunities and completely new types of companies that can integrate and analyze market data. Such organizations have ample knowledge on goods and services, customers and suppliers, customer expectations that can be identified and analyzed. Although the word "big data" is fairly recent, the process of gathering and processing vast volumes of information for subsequent review is age-old.
